This site was created initially as a creative outlet for a young reader and turned into a space where

About Mary

Mary enjoys primarily fiction, fantasy and anything involving magic. But occasionally enjoys a good
murder mystery or how-to books. By day she works at a Milwaukee software company and by night she can
be found playing with her dog Lana (named after Lana Kane), traveling the world, or of course curled up
reading a good book.
